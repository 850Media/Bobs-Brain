## The Pain Points It Solves

### For Business Owners / Managers
| Pain | How FieldTrain Solves It |
|------|--------------------------|
| Training is expensive | Self-serve quizzes, no trainer needed |
| Creating training content is time-consuming | Upload docs, AI does the rest |
| Hard to track who's trained on what | Dashboard with completion tracking |
| Compliance requires training documentation | Built-in records + certificates |
| Workers forget what they learned | Gamification encourages repeat engagement |

### For Field Workers
| Pain | How FieldTrain Solves It |
|------|--------------------------|
| Training is boring | Gamified, competitive, bite-sized |
| Feel like they're being "tested" | Feels like a game, not a test |
| No recognition for learning | Leaderboards, badges, certificates |
| Training cuts into work time | Mobile, do it during downtime |

## How It Works

### Back Office (Admin)
1. **Upload content** ‚Äî PDFs, docs, articles, product manuals, SOPs
2. **AI processes** ‚Äî Extracts key concepts, generates quiz questions
3. **Review/customize** ‚Äî Admin can tweak questions if needed
4. **Assign to staff** ‚Äî Push to individuals, teams, or all staff
5. **Track progress** ‚Äî Dashboard shows completion, scores, trends

### Field Staff (Mobile App)
1. **Get notification** ‚Äî New quiz available
2. **Take quiz** ‚Äî Mobile-friendly, quick (5-10 questions)
3. **See results** ‚Äî Instant feedback, correct answers shown
4. **Check leaderboard** ‚Äî See ranking vs. peers
5. **Earn certificate** ‚Äî Download/email upon completion

## Key Features

### Content Processing (AI)
- Upload any written content (PDF, Word, text)
- AI extracts key concepts and facts
- Generates multiple choice, true/false, short answer questions
- **Guardrails:** Questions are strictly based on uploaded content (no hallucination)

### Quiz Experience
- Mobile-first design
- Bite-sized (5-10 questions per quiz)
- Timed option for urgency
- Immediate feedback with explanations
- Can retake to improve score

### Gamification
- **Leaderboards** ‚Äî Weekly, monthly, all-time
- **Badges** ‚Äî "Safety Pro", "Product Expert", etc.
- **Streaks** ‚Äî Daily/weekly engagement rewards
- **Points** ‚Äî Accumulate across quizzes
- **Teams** ‚Äî Department vs. department competition

### Tracking & Compliance
- Individual progress reports
- Team/company dashboards
- Completion timestamps (proof of training)
- Score history
- Export for compliance audits

### Certificates
- Auto-generated upon passing
- Customizable template (company branding)
- Email to employee
- PDF for HR files
- QR code for verification (optional)
